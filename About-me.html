Kia ora! My name is Ari. I'm a law graduate, traveller, aspiring polyglot and self-help addict.
After travelling I realised that I was not cut out for legal practice or a 9-5 grind for that matter.
My passion is travel and I decided to do this course because it gives me the ability to live and work remotely as I live my authentic truth and discover the hidden corners of this planet.
I'm a creative person and my intention is that with this course, I will gain powerful tools of creation to travel the world...and maybe even change it.
