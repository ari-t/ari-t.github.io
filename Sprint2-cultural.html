My strengths include my creatvity, my burst of energy and my ability to learn quickly.
My limitations are my ability to keep on task in a timely manner without getting distracted by issues that pop up. I have a lot of creative ideas that I have trouble editing down. How and where you will seek help in a timely way.

By being active in slack and the groups I'm apart of. Also by seeking out help on the internet when necessary.
